The Florida Home Protection Plan

South Florida's subtropical climate keeps pests active year-round. Our premier quarterly service program is designed to build a continuous barrier around your property, intercepting insects exterior-first before they enter your home.

With our regular scheduled visits and complete service warranty, you can enjoy peace of mind knowing your home is shielded from ants, roaches, silverfish, and spiders. If pests ever break through the barrier, we return and treat the area for free.

Plan Details

What is Included in Your Plan?

A comprehensive, multi-layer pest defense program applied on a consistent seasonal schedule.

🛡️

1. Perimeter Barrier Shield

We apply a long-lasting protective liquid and granular barrier around your home's foundation to intercept foraging ants, roaches, and spiders before they reach your doors and windows.

🧹

2. Web Sweep & De-Webbing

Our technicians sweep down wasp nests and spider webs from your eaves, soffits, and window frames during every scheduled quarterly visit, keeping your exterior looking clean.

🏠

3. Custom Interior Treatment

Should you ever notice pest activity inside your home, we treat structural cracks, crevices, plumbing penetrations, and wall voids using targeted, low-toxicity gel baits and dusts.

🔄

4. Free Re-Service Calls

Our plan is fully backed by our service guarantee. If crawling pests return between scheduled quarterly visits, simply call our desk and we will re-treat free of charge.

🐜

5. Covered Pests

The plan covers common South Florida household crawlers: ghost ants, carpenter ants, silverfish, spiders, crickets, earwigs, and American cockroaches (palmetto bugs).

➕

6. Optional Plan Add-ons

Customize your home protection with specialized upgrades: Mosquito suppression, Rodent baiting/monitoring stations, or Termite inspection warranties.
