Weston Service FAQs

Frequently asked questions regarding pest services, permitting, and response times in Weston.

How does the proximity to the Everglades affect Weston pest pressure?

The Everglades is a massive natural reservoir of wildlife and insects. During wet seasons, rising waters push rodents, spiders, and ants outward into Weston's landscaped developments in search of dry shelter.

Do you offer organic or low-impact pest control in Weston?

Yes, we offer green and low-impact options. Our Integrated Pest Management (IPM) framework is designed to minimize chemical exposure, making it ideal for Weston's eco-conscious community.

How do you protect Weston crawlspaces and attics from roof rats?

We perform full structural exclusion audits, sealing all soffit vents, roof junctions, and pipe entries with chew-proof metal wiring and sealants to prevent rats from nesting in your attic.
