FAQ

Specials & Offers Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about redeeming discounts and pest protection packages.

Do pest control specials apply to new customers?

Yes! We provide targeted initial service discounts to help new residential and commercial clients establish active barriers. Call our team to confirm current introductory specials for your area.

Can I combine pest control offers?

Generally, promotional offers and initial service specials cannot be combined with other active discounts or coupons. Please review the fine print or consult our team during scheduling.

Do you offer termite inspection specials?

Yes, we offer free initial visual termite assessments for qualifying South Florida homeowners. This allows us to inspect perimeter crawlspaces and foundations before proposing target barrier systems.

Are there mosquito control specials in South Florida?

Yes! We offer seasonal mosquito control specials to suppress yard breeding cycles during wet months. Contact us to learn about monthly packages and one-time event spraying specials.

Do you offer commercial pest control discounts?

Yes, we customize commercial pest management assessments for restaurants, warehouses, HOAs, and managed properties, incorporating specialized initial onboarding discounts.

How do I redeem a special?

Simply select your preferred offer, call our Fort Lauderdale office, or request service online through our Specials page form. Mention the offer during your call or initial consultation before scheduling.

Are specials available outside Fort Lauderdale?

Our service specials apply to all qualifying Broward County and South Florida service areas, including Plantation, Weston, Hollywood, Davie, Pembroke Pines, and Dania Beach. Call us to confirm coverage for your zip code.
