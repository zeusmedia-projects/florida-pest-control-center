Our Story

Family-Owned, Locally Driven Pest Protection

Florida Pest Control Center is an independent, family-owned company. We were built on a simple principle: providing South Florida properties with the structural defense and service depth of a regional operator, while keeping the personal touch, transparency, and integrity of a local trades team.

We live and work in Broward County. Our team understands that Florida's tropical pest pressure isn't a seasonal occurrence—it is a year-round reality. That's why we focus on advanced barriers and Integrated Pest Management (IPM), rather than just spraying chemicals indoors on a one-off basis.
