Pest Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding scheduling, warranties, safety, and Florida-specific pest treatments.

How often should pest control service happen in Florida?

For most residential properties, we recommend regular quarterly pest control (every 3 months). Florida's tropical heat and high humidity break down protective exterior barriers over time. Consistent quarterly treatments ensure that barriers remain active, stopping pests outside before they migrate inside.

Are your pest treatments safe for kids and pets?

Yes. The safety of your family and pets is our top priority. We use low-toxicity products applied in strict accordance with EPA regulations. Our primary treatments are applied to the exterior perimeter, keeping products outside your living spaces. If indoor treatments are required, we focus on cracks and crevices inaccessible to children and pets.

What is covered under the Service Satisfaction Promise?

Our quarterly protection plans come with a Service Satisfaction Promise. If crawling pests return to your home between scheduled visits, simply call us. We will send a local technician to treat the problem areas. Re-service support may be available on qualifying plans.

What is the termite warranty?

We provide termite warranty options for qualifying homes treated by our team. If a termite infestation is detected during the warranty period, we will perform necessary spot treatments or complete applications to treat the active problem. Ask your technician about warranty options available for your specific treatment plan.
